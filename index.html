Hospital Medicine Catalog

Find information about medicines available at our hospital

Available Medicines

Paracetamol

Uses: Pain relief, fever reduction

Dosage: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours

Amoxicillin

Uses: Bacterial infections treatment

Dosage: 250-500mg three times daily

Ibuprofen

Uses: Pain relief, inflammation reduction

Dosage: 200-400mg every 4-6 hours

Omeprazole

Uses: Reduces stomach acid, treats GERD

Dosage: 20mg once daily

Loratadine

Uses: Allergy relief, antihistamine

Dosage: 10mg once daily

Aspirin

Uses: Pain relief, blood thinner

Dosage: 75-325mg daily

About Our Hospital

Our qualified pharmacists are available to provide consultation and advice on medications.
